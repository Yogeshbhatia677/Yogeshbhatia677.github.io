What is the difference between Cleaning and Disinfecting?

Though cleaning and disinfecting seem the same, they are two entirely different things.

  • Cleaning is an act of removing contaminants from the surface
  • Disinfecting is an act of killing pathogens

High-touch surfaces to clean and disinfect:

  • Doorknobs
  • Tables
  • Dining chairs (seat, back, and arms)
  • Kitchen Counters
  • Washroom counters
  • Faucets and knobs
  • Toilet seat and handles

How to Clean and disinfect:

  1. Firstly, clean the surfaces, eliminating any contaminants, dust, or remains. You can do this by wiping them with soap-infused water and a towel.
  2. Then apply a disinfectant according to the surface. The fastest and effortless way to do this is with disinfecting wipes or disinfectant spray.
